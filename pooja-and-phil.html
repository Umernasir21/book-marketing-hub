The strategy

Four weeks. One story.
An audience that can't wait.

Each week builds on the last — from curiosity to emotion to community. By Week 4, your readers aren't just followers. They're invested.

Week 1
Spark the Curiosity
Reveal the book, introduce the characters, and share the author's personal story. Make people stop scrolling.
Week 2
Build the Emotional Hook
Character deep-dives and powerful quote cards that make readers feel something real. Tag-a-friend energy.
Week 3
Deepen the Connection
Themes, first-chapter teasers, and Phil's voice. Turn casual scrollers into people who genuinely care about this story.
Week 4
Build Urgency & Community
Cultural celebration, audience polls, and a heartfelt thank-you. Warm them up for the pre-order announcement.
