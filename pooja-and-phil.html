The strategy

Four weeks. One story.
An audience that can't wait.

Each week builds on the last — from curiosity to emotion to community. By Week 4, your readers aren't just followers. They're invested.

Week 1
Spark the Curiosity
Reveal the book, introduce the characters, and share the author's personal story. Make people stop scrolling.
Week 2
Build the Emotional Hook
Character deep-dives and powerful quote cards that make readers feel something real. Tag-a-friend energy.
Week 3
Deepen the Connection
Themes, first-chapter teasers, and Phil's voice. Turn casual scrollers into people who genuinely care about this story.
Week 4
Build Urgency & Community
Cultural celebration, audience polls, and a heartfelt thank-you. Warm them up for the pre-order announcement.

Wednesday — Day 24
Reader Question: Would You Take This Job?
Audience Engage
Facebook Instagram BookTok
Caption — Ready to Publish
Here's the scenario: You desperately need money to fund your dream. The only job available is working as a personal assistant for a grumpy, wheelchair-bound, 70-year-old CEO who has already sent two assistants running. Do you take it? 🤔 → Option A: YES — money is money. Dream first. → Option B: NO — my mental health matters more. → Option C: I'd take it… and try to win him over. 😂 This is exactly the decision Pooja faces in Chapter 1. Comment your answer below — I'm reading every single one. 👇 #WouldYouRather #PoojaAndPhil #InteractivePost #ReadingCommunity #BookTok #CharacterDecisions #BansiPattni
Designer Brief
Bold, fun question graphic. Orange headline text: "Would YOU take this job?" Three large answer buttons styled boldly (A, B, C) with short punchy labels. Pooja silhouette (dancing) in corner as visual anchor. Clean white/cream background. For TikTok: "POV: you need the money" style video. Author or character voice-over reading the scenario. On-screen text poll with A/B/C options. Fun, slightly dramatic energy. Encourage comments.
