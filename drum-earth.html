Drum Earth Trilogy
A.J. Hugo Parker — Drum Earth Publishing
When Daniel goes to university, he doesn't realise his fate is already sealed. Spanning from the Lascaux caves 20,000 years ago to 2070, this extraordinary trilogy weaves past lives, spirit worlds, ecological destiny and one of the most unique love stories in modern fiction.
Past Lives Spirit World Ecological Thriller Paranormal Fiction Trilogy Published
📖
Three Books. One Journey.
Book 1 — Daniel at university discovers spirit worlds. Book 2 — Love, loss, and a rescue mission in the Middle World. Book 3 — Spanning 33 years, Orlando and Dorian help Daniel fulfil his ecological destiny.
🌍
20,000 Years of Spirit Cycles
From Lascaux caves in 20,000 BPT to Canterbury in 1420, Mexico City in 1521, to modern England and a 2070 epilogue — the story of DRUM EARTH spans millennia of reincarnation and consequence.
✨
Unique Characters
Daniel, Francesca, Maisy, Stephen, Orlando, Dorian — each carrying the weight of past-life spirits. Animal guides: Panther, Bear, Horse, Eagle, Peregrine Falcon and Eagle Owl.
🌿
The Call to DRUM EARTH
At its heart, Drum Earth is a call to action — to save the planet from ecological disaster. The ORLANDOR Company's special trees and the 2070 epilogue make this a uniquely hopeful and visionary story.
★ Marketing Approach — Published Book
Unlike pre-launch marketing, every post in this calendar drives readers to buy, review, and share the book on Amazon and Goodreads right now. The strategy focuses on discoverability, social proof (reviews), and building a reader community around the trilogy. Amazon reviews are gold — this calendar is built around generating them.
The strategy

Four Weeks to Build
a Reader Community

The book is live. Now the goal is to find its readers, earn reviews, and build word-of-mouth. Each week has a clear mission.

Week 1
Announce & Drive to Amazon
Tell the world it's live. Introduce the world of Drum Earth. Every post ends with a direct link to buy on Amazon.
Week 2
Hook with the Story
Character reveals, first-chapter teasers, and the spirit world concept. Make readers desperate to know what happens.
Week 3
Social Proof & Reviews
Request Amazon reviews actively. Engage readers. Share early praise. Reviews = discoverability = sales.
Week 4
Community & Momentum
Deep-dives on themes (ecology, past lives, spirit animals). Build a loyal community of readers who become ambassadors.

Maximise your reach

Amazon & Goodreads
Power Tips

⭐
Reviews = Visibility
Amazon's algorithm rewards books with reviews. Even 10–15 reviews significantly boost discoverability. Ask every reader directly.
🔗
Link in Bio Always
Every single post should have "Amazon link in bio." Change your bio link to the Amazon page and keep it there permanently.
📚
Goodreads Profile
Set up an author profile on Goodreads. Add the book. Ask readers to add it to their "Want to Read" shelf — this builds organic visibility.
🎯
Target Hashtags
#BookTok #PastLives #SpiritualFiction #ParanormalRomance #EcologicalFiction #Reincarnation — these are your core discovery tags.
